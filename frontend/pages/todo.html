Planned Features

  • - Advanced Search: multi-criteria search with author, topic, venue, and date filters
  • - Recommendations: retrieve paper recommendations from the Semantic Scholar API
  • - Citation Analysis: deep citation network analysis with impact metrics
  • - Timeline Views: chronological paper timeline with research evolution tracking
  • - Personal Library: save, organize, and annotate papers in custom collections
  • - Export & Sharing: export citations, share collections, and collaborate with others
  • - Live Data Sources: integration with arXiv, PubMed, IEEE, and other major databases
  • - Full Content: fetch paper content from arXiv LaTeX submissions
